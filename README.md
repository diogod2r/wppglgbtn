
## 🟩 Google Button WhatsApp

**Easily send Google search results to WhatsApp with one click!**
Google Button WhatsApp adds a convenient button to Google search pages, allowing you to quickly share search results directly to WhatsApp Web.

---


---

### 🚀 What It Does

* Adds a WhatsApp button to Google search result pages.
* Lets you send selected search results or links to WhatsApp Web instantly.
* Works 100% locally in your browser — no data is sent anywhere.

Share search results with friends or colleagues in just one click!

---


---

### ⚙️ How It Works

The extension runs locally in your browser, monitoring Google search result pages. When you click the WhatsApp button:

1. The selected search result or link is extracted.
2. WhatsApp Web is opened (or focused) with the message ready to send.
3. No information is sent to any external server.

---


---

### 🔒 Privacy

Google Button WhatsApp **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data.
It only interacts with Google search result pages and WhatsApp Web in your browser.
